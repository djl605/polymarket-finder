- Hendrickson is one of the few elite-ish edge options left;
- a fresh insider rumor specifically tied Baltimore to Hendrickson;
- and the resolution window is long enough that Baltimore only needs to be his eventual next team by August 31, not necessarily immediately.

That said, **64.3%** still implies something stronger than “favorite.” It implies Baltimore is more likely than **all non-Baltimore outcomes combined**, including every other team and “Other.” In a multi-team NFL free-agent market with no official deal, age/injury concerns, and reported price disagreement, that is a demanding threshold.

Because volume is low, I would not treat this as a highly efficient market-clearing consensus. The tight spread only shows current posted willingness near that price; it does not prove broad participation.
